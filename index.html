🎯 Our Mission
TradeTrack was built for traders who are serious about improving. Most traders fail not because of bad strategies — but because they don't track, reflect, or stay disciplined. We built this to change that.

Every feature — from the PnL calendar to discipline rules to the XP system — is designed to make you a more consistent, self-aware trader.
